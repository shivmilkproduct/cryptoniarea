Beyond the community, we protect real organizations

Cryptonic Area isn't only about training members. We also work directly with businesses and organizations — small or large — to secure their systems, harden their networks, and support investigations when something goes wrong.

Business & Organization Security

End-to-end security for organizations of any size — from small businesses to large enterprises — covering infrastructure, applications and data.

Network Security

Assessing, hardening and monitoring networks so organizations stay protected against real, active threats.

Cyber Crime Investigation
