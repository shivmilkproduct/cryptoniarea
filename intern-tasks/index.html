Before You Start

How this internship actually works

Four things to understand before you open a single project.

We're not spoon-feeding you

No live, step-by-step "watch me do it" sessions. Every project comes with a full explanation of what to do and what to submit — the execution, the research, and the figuring-it-out is yours. That's exactly what real cyber security work looks like.

AI is allowed — copy-paste isn't

Use AI to understand a concept, learn a tool, or unblock yourself — that's smart use of what's available. But the analysis, the decisions, and the report have to be genuinely yours. You'll need to explain your own work later.

"What did you learn?" — be ready

If your batch is called for a review, the question won't be about completion — it'll be about understanding. Focus on actually learning the concept behind each project, not just finishing it and moving on.

Top performers get recognized

Interns who go beyond the minimum — deep research, industry-level reporting, clean execution — get featured and recognized on our official platform. The certificate is guaranteed at 3/5; recognition is earned.

Certificates aren't the point — they'll come once you complete any 3 of 5 projects with proper documentation. The actual point is the skill. Take your time, research properly, and use every project as a chance to genuinely get better at cyber security — not just to submit something and move on.
